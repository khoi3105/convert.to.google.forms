Welcome, this is the official page of our extension Convert To Google Forms™. These are the informations that about our add-on
Why was this add-on born?

Practice (multiple choice questions) many times is the best solution (best) to be able to firmly grasp the knowledge learned. However, the problem is how can students practice in any period of time without consuming too much time of the teacher. We realize that there are many add-ons available in the market, but they are inconvenient to use.

What is our solution?

We have created an add-on that works on top of the Google Docs™ platform, which when active creates a form in Google Forms™ with pre-provided multiple choice questions and answers. Fast conversion time, using just 1 click are the advantages of the software.

Question: What will we do with your data?

A: We do not collect your information or data. When this add-on is up and running, we will just take the data in the Google Docs™ file and convert it into Google Forms™
